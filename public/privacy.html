Privacy Policy

ClearTab AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension operates with a privacy-first approach:

No data collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data to external servers.

No tracking: We do not track your browsing activity, history, or behavior.

No data selling: We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data.

Local processing: All content summarization is processed locally or through secure API calls. No content is stored permanently.

Usage statistics are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted externally.
